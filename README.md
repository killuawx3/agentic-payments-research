# Agentic Payments Research

Research hub for onchain agentic payment solutions and the role of blockchain/stablecoins in agentic commerce.

## What This Repo Covers

- **Onchain agentic payment protocols** — How AI agents transact autonomously using crypto rails
- **Stablecoin infrastructure for agents** — USDC, USDT, and novel stablecoin designs for machine-to-machine payments
- **Agentic commerce patterns** — Emerging models where AI agents buy, sell, negotiate, and settle on behalf of users
- **Protocol deep dives** — Technical breakdowns of existing solutions (Skyfire, Locus, Coinbase AgentKit, etc.)
- **Comparative analysis** — Feature matrices, trade-offs, and gap analysis across the landscape
